    Admission Requirements



    General requirements from C-Certificate to Bachelor Degree




    Christian University College Welcomes prospective students who have never been admitted to a university are considered for admission as freshmement,
    Admission requirements for C-Certificate to Bachelor Degree include:

    • A completed Application Form
    • WAEC/WASSCE Certificate
    • High School Diploma
    • High school Transcript
    • Two Letters of Recommendation (One from the school last attended and the other from religion Institution)
    • Personal Essay of why you want to attend C.U.C and future goals (300 words)
    • Three passport photos
    • Recent Medical Certificate
    • Birth Certicate or Passport

    Exception from entry Examination: Students earning Division I or Division II passing scrores on the West African Examination Counil (WAEC) Exam are elible
    for admission and do not have to sit the placement examination. Each successful applicant receives a Letter of Acceptance to enroll at the University.
    Admitted students must enroll within the two consecutive semesters immediately following the date of the letter of acceptance or restart the application process.



    Graduate Admission



    Requirements

    • A complete Application Form
    • Minimum of 4 years Bachelor Degree or Equivalent Certicate
    • Transcript (GPA of 2.0 and above)
    • Two Letters of Recommendation (One from the school last attended and the other from religion Institution)
    • Personal statement detailing reasons for selecting C.U.C and expected contribution to the university and communnity life(500 words)
    • Three passport photos
    • Recent Medical Certificate
    • Birth Certicate or Passport
    • An interview with the Chair of the Department and/or Dean of the College to which admission is being requested

    Application Deadlines:

    June 15 for Semester I
    October 15 for Semester II

    PhD. Admission Requirements
    • A completed Application Form
    • Official Graduate Degree
    • Official Graduate Transcript
    • Official Bachelor Degree or Equivalent
    • Official Bachelor Degree Transcript
    • Three Letters of Recommendation (One from Professor of previous university, employer and religion Institution)
    • Personal statement detailing reasons for selecting C.U.C and expected contribution to the university and communnity life(500 words)
    • Research Proposal (200 words)
    • Three passport photos
    • Recent Medical Certificate
    • Birth Certicate or Passport
    • An interview with the Chair of the Department and/or Dean of the College to which admission is being requested.

    Application Deadlines:

    June 15 for Semester I
    October 15 for Semester II
